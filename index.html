Urban_OS is an experimental and design fiction research project started with the goal of developing new theories and approaches for the “smart city.”

This has been an ongoing research project since October of 2019 carried out through reading literature, conducting interviews, making.

It presents three case studies that critically explore and present new approaches to computing in urban space through augmented reality technologies.
